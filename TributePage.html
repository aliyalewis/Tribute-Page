Dr. Diarra Blue


Picture of Dr. Diarra Blue with a Lemur


Dr. Diarra Blue is an American Veterinarian who practice his skill in Texas. Although saving animals is part of what he does at the hospital that has made him popular too. He features in the TV show “The Vet Life” alongside Dr. Aubrey Ross and Dr. Micheal Lavigne. The show chronicles their juggling act running the full-service hospital and animal shelter while managing family lives filled with spouses, parents, in-laws, children, pets, and friends.
